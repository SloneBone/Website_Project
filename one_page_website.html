Adopt Today

Welcome to The_Dog_House.org!

We are a no kill shelter! We usually house about 10 dogs maximum in our home. We take these dogs into our dog-sanctuary for as long as it takes. Our hope is that we can rehabilitate these dogs while we find them the perfect family and home!

Have a look at the pictures of the dogs and if you like one please contact us to start the adoption process. Our staff will get back to you in 24 hours or less. Thank you for your interest in giving these pups a new home!!
